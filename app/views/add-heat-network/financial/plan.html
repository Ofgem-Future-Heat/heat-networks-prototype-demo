Help with CSCPs

At a minimum these should include:

Supplier information

To help reduce disruption to customers during the onboarding process, in the event of a step-in:

  • details of arrangements with third-party service providers to ensure continuity of services
  • billing system information
  • customer numbers and information about their payment methods
  • Priority Services Register (PSR) customer list

Key contacts

To ensure individuals are aware of their responsibilities in the event of supplier failure:

  • details of key staff, including directors, heads of teams and senior officers
  • details of key contacts at service providers

Customer account information

To ensure, that a potential new supplier is aware of critical customer account information, in the event of a step-in:

  • details of the processes that would be followed to prepare a summary of customer debt information and customer account balances

Data

To ensure there's a smooth transition for customers to a new supplier, in the event of a step-in:

  • details of how to access data sets, and where data sets are held
  • details of how the supplier proposes to keep its data sets up to date
  • details of methodologies for handing over information and customer data

Assets

Details of the key physical and contractual assets.
