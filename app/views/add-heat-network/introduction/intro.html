Add a heat network - introduction

{{title}}

  • You must answer a short series of questions giving context for your heat network.
  • We'll then let you know your specific obligations, and what further information we need.
  • After this, you'll be able to save your progress at any point.
  • You must then provide the remaining information for your heat network, and submit it to Ofgem for processing.

Initial information you'll need

To help us understand your specific obligations, you'll first need to tell us:

  • if your organisation undertakes operator or supplier activities on the network
  • if the network is currently supplying customers, and - if so - the year it started supplying
  • if the network does or will supply more than one building
  • if the network is or will be a 'self-supply' network
  • if the network does or will supply microbusinesses

We'll provide definitions and guidance for any possibly unfamiliar concepts as you progress.
